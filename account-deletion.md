# MYONE Account and Data Deletion

**Last updated:** 2 June 2026

This page explains how MYONE users can delete their account and request deletion of MYONE cloud data.

## Delete from Inside the App

If you can still open MYONE:

1. Open MYONE.
2. Go to **Settings > Data**.
3. Tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm the deletion prompt.

This deletes your Firebase sign-in, MYONE cloud sync data, entitlement records, feedback history, and local data on that device.

Shared wallet records may remain visible to other wallet members, but your account profile is removed or anonymized.

## Request Deletion After Uninstalling

If you no longer have the app installed, email:

**dailyapp.support@gmail.com**

Use the subject:

> MYONE account deletion request

Include the Google account email you used to sign in to MYONE.

We may ask you to verify that you control that email address before deleting cloud data.

## What Is Deleted

- Firebase Authentication account tied to MYONE.
- Personal cloud sync data under your MYONE user ID.
- FCM notification token records.
- MYONE entitlement records.
- Feedback reports tied to your account.
- AI, receipt scan, crypto, and feedback usage counters tied to your account.

## What May Remain

- Local data on devices where MYONE is still installed until you delete the app or use in-app deletion.
- Google Drive backup files you created with MYONE, because those files are stored in your own Google Drive. You can delete them from Google Drive.
- Shared wallet transaction history needed by other wallet members. Your account identity is removed or anonymized where possible.
- Operational logs needed for security, fraud prevention, debugging, and legal compliance, retained for up to 90 days unless a longer period is legally required.

## Timeline

We aim to respond within **7 business days**.

Verified deletion requests are normally completed within **30 days**.
